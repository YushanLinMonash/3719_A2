Australia Energy Consumption

Visual analysis of energy consumption and related economic data in Australia

Electricity Income by State

The map visualizes electricity income by state in Australia, with a color scale representing the income levels. States with higher income are darker.

Energy Consumption by Type

This stacked bar chart displays the breakdown of energy consumption by different types of energy sources.

Energy Consumption by Industry and State

This heat map shows the energy consumption by different industries across various states in Australia.

Energy Consumption Comparison by Industry

The radar chart compares energy consumption across industries in Australia.

Energy Consumption vs GDP

This graph shows energy consumption per capita vs. GDP across Australian states. The bubble size represents population, and the color represents the state.

Renewable Energy Isotype

This isotype chart visualizes the use of renewable energy in Australia.

Comparison of Renewable and Non-Renewable Energy

This chart compares the usage of renewable vs. non-renewable energy sources in Australia using concatenated isotype representations.
